Wedding Visual Suite

This project is a complete wedding visual suite designed for Mojisola and Damilare, focused on creating a refined and cohesive look across all wedding materials. The design blends soft tones, elegant typography, and subtle decorative details to reflect the warmth and intimacy of the celebration.

The suite includes invitation cards, thank you cards, keepsakes, and supporting print pieces, all carefully coordinated to maintain visual consistency. Each element was designed to feel timeless and personal while ensuring clarity, readability, and print readiness.

Together, the visuals create a seamless wedding experience that communicates love, tradition, and celebration through thoughtful and intentional design.
