Tether USDT Web3 Carousel Design

This project explores a futuristic visual direction for Tether (USDT), focused on stability, speed, and trust within the Web3 space. Dark tones, green accents, and abstract elements are used to create a modern, premium feel while clearly communicating the core message of reliable digital finance.

The carousel was designed for social media, turning complex crypto ideas into clear, scroll-friendly visuals. Strong hierarchy, bold headlines, and consistent styling help keep the content easy to understand and visually cohesive across all slides.
